About

Add players by tapping the add player button. Scores can be inputed directly or as differences to the current score. For instance, if a player has a score of 100 and we type +20 on the score field for that player, the score will be updated to 120. Similarly, if we enter -50 the score will now be 70. To enter negative scores one can enter 0 and then the negative score, since a minus sign in front of a number is always interpreted as a score delta.

Scores are saved even if you don't add the app to your home screen.
